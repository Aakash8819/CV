1. Density Based Traffic Control using IR Sensor
    • The aim is to realize smooth motion of cars in the transportation routes.
    • Proposed a system based on Arduino microcontroller that evaluates the traffic density using IR sensors and accomplishes dynamic timing slots with different levels.
    • A portable controller device is designed to solve the problem of emergency vehicles stuck in the overcrowded roads.
  2. SMART CAR PARKING
    • Smart parking is a solution to metropolitan cities to reduce congestion, cut vehicle emission totals and save a person's time by helping them in finding a spot to park.
    • It is a parking system, usually a new one that is equipped with special structured devices (things) to detect the available parking slots at any parking area.
    • It is a Real-Time environment that has sensors and devices embedded into parking spaces, transmitting data on the occupancy status and making the driver know the status of available space.
